Although it may seem that Snake is not anything too complex, certainly not enough to warrant a full in-depth narrative paper; one could argue that it is more than what meets the eye. The game's meaning and the feeling it provokes lie beyond its simple mechanics. Snake game was first invented by computer programmer Taneli Armanto for the Nokia in 1997. Snake was built on the concept game Blockage (1976), a two-player arcade game with similar game mechanics. The goal of the game is simple: eat and grow, but don't crash into yourself or the wall, YEEEEEOWWWWCHHH!!!!!
